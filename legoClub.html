LEGO Club Engineering Challenge

  • Get Ready! Set! Build!
  • Creativity abounds the 1st Tuesday of each month at the Jack Russell Memorial Library, as area kids come together to take on the HUHS Robotics Team 1091’s monthly Engineering Challenge.
  • Each month, Team 1091 introduces a new S.T.E.M.-themed challenge to attendees. They then have 60 minutes to design and complete the challenge using LEGOs. Every attendees’ construction is then displayed in the library’s Children’s Area for the remainder of the month. Previous challenges have included candy pumpkin launchers, marble runs, and wind cars.
  • LEGO Club is open to preschoolers on up. No registration is needed to attend.
  • Location: Jack Russell Memorial Library 100 Park Ave Hartford, WI
  • Time: 3:30 – 4:30 pm
