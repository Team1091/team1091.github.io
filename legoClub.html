Countdown to Lego Club

LEGO Club Engineering Challenge

  • Get Ready! Set! Build!
  • Creativity abounds the 1st Tuesday 3:30-4:30pm of each month at the Jack Russell Memorial Library.
  • Each month, Team 1091 introduces a new S.T.E.M.-themed challenge to attendees.
  • They then have 60 minutes to design and complete the challenge using LEGOs.
  • Every attendees’ construction is then displayed in the Library’s Children’s Area for the remainder of the month.
  • LEGO Club is open to preschoolers on up. No registration is needed to attend.
  • Location: Jack Russell Memorial Library 100 Park Ave Hartford, WI

History

  • In 2022, Team 1091 began a partnership with the Jack Russell Memorial Library to lead the library’s monthly LEGO Club.
